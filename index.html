About Me

I am currently a programmer at Cielostar, Inc in Minneapolis, MN. My role could best be described as a Full Stack .NET Developer. I maintain and add features to VB.NET and C# MVC.NET web applications built on T-SQL databases. I regularly work with C#, VB.NET, Transact SQL and JavaScript, as well as their relative markup languages such as ASPX, CSHTML and HTML. I am extremely familiar with Visual Studio, SQL Server Management Studio and Team Foundation Server, in addition to a number of other programs. I have a natural aptitude for technology, as well as figuring out how things, both programmatic and physical, are made, repaired, and how they work, which makes me a great fit for many development teams.

My hobbies could best be summed up as making things. This includes 3D printing, woodworking, model making, and basically any other skill or tool needed to accomplish my goal. 3D printing quickly led to the desire to model my own parts, so I taught myself Autodesk Fusion 360, and now consider myself competent in its use. I just generally enjoy assembling, disassembling and repairing things. Seeing how an object goes together, works and can be recreated just comes naturally to me, and it is a skill I embrace on a daily bases.
